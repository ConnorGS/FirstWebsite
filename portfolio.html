My pong project in Scratch! Egg Boss fight!

I made 2 games in scratch. One game is a version of pong with 2 players fighting each other and the ball getting faster the more times the ball is hit. The second game is a boss battle survival game. In this game you have to survive 20 rounds against a bat boss throwing eggs at you and you only have 3 lifes.
